My Story

Developing in JavaScript

In 11th grade, I first started working with JavaScript. It's made me a far better programmer and has helped me find my ground in the coding community.

Ever since that year, I've never stopped coding in JavaScript. And whether it's for school or for personal projects, I still loving coding in it to this day.
